Attempts to write an entry to unknown_protocols.log, rate-limited to avoid spamming the log with duplicates.

Parameters
analyzerThe name of the analyzer that was trying to forward the packet.
protocolThe protocol of the next header that couldn't be forwarded.

lenThe remaining length of the data in the packet being processed.
