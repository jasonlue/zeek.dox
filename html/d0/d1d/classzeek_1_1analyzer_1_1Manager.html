Detailed Description

Class maintaining and scheduling available protocol analyzers.

The manager maintains a registry of all available protocol analyzers, including a mapping between their textual names and Tag. It instantantiates new analyzers on demand. For new connections, the manager sets up their initial analyzer tree, including adding the right PIA, respecting well-known ports, and tracking any analyzers specifically scheduled for individidual connections.
